My Qualities

Super Speed

I'm the fastest thing alive! I once ran through the entire multiverse in an hour. There's that other time when I stopped time for everyone else while I still was able to move normally.

Leadership

I started the Sonic Team in 2000 when I met Knuckles, Amy and Tails. In 2017 I started a rebellion group to stop Infinite and Eggman's plans.

Sociable

I made a lot of friends along the way. I'm not afraid to risk my reputation if it is for the better good.

Optimistic

I always try to see the bright side of things. Everything has a solution in my eyes.

Flexible

I'm really flexible, I can work whenver I can or want. When I do something, I'm really dedicated to it.

Justice

I have a strong sense of right and wrong. I don't tolerate bullying, cruelty or exploitation. I always stand up for those who can't defend themselves.
