My Experiences

1991: The start of Everything

1991 was when it all began. I zipped onto the scene to save innocent animals from Robotnik's crazy contraptions. Green Hills Zone? Iconic. Chaos Emerlads? Collected. Speed? Unmatched.

1992: A friend on the Journey

Things were getting tougher, and then I met Tails. He was just this little fox with two tails spinning around like a helicopter.

1993: Race Through Time

This year was... different. Somehow, Robotnik got his hands on a machine that let him mess with time itself. To fix things, I had to run faster than ever before, traveling between the past, present, and future to stop Robotnik from turning paradise into a wasteland.

1994: Knuckles

Ah, Knuckles. At first, he was a pain. Robotnik tricked him into thinking I was the bad guy, so he kept getting in my way. But when the truth came out, he turned out to be a pretty solid dude. Stubborn as a rock, but reliable. Together, we stopped Robotnik’s plans again, and for a moment, it felt like we’d finally won.

1999: A New Dimension

Everything changed when I discovered new worlds, cities full of people, temples with ancient secrets, and creatures beyond anything I’d ever seen. I met Chaos, this water-like being, and Shadow, a rival with a mysterious past. It was overwhelming, but it reminded me why I keep moving forward.

2001: Shadows and Sacrifices

Shadow... He wasn’t just a rival; he was someone carrying a burden I couldn’t imagine. For all his strength, he had this deep sadness. Fighting alongside him, I realized we weren’t so different. We both wanted to protect what mattered, even if we had different ways of doing it.

2006: A Misstep

There was a time I felt like I couldn’t catch a break. Everything I tried seemed to go wrong. It was frustrating, like I was tripping over my own feet. But even when the world felt heavy, I kept running. That’s all I know how to do.

2010: Rediscovering Joy

Things started to feel right again. I stopped worrying so much about what people expected and just focused on the joy of running, exploring, and helping wherever I could. The world was still full of surprises, and I was ready for all of them.

2020: Lights, camera, Action!

I never imagined I’d end up in the movies. Suddenly, everyone knew who I was, even people who’d never seen a loop-de-loop before. It was weird, but kind of awesome. Seeing people cheer for me again? That was the best.
